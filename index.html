Samuel Amanya holds a Bachelors of Arts in Economics from University of California, Berkeley. The areas of emphasis are in Economic Theory, Econometrics, Microeconomics, Macroeconomics and Experimental Economics. He is also a Mastercard Foundation Scholar from Rwanda, and a reciepient of several other academic awards.

A lifelong learner, Samuel is curious and collaborative, and holds an egalitarian outlook on life. He also believes in the concept of "opportunity meeting preparation", which means he is highly action oriented, a self-starter, and results driven.

He is a ferocious reader, believing that there is a lot to learn in books as they are direct portions of great minds.
